# Claim ledger

Use `claim-ledger/` for proposed claims before they move into chapter briefs or
manuscript prose.

Create a claim note when analysis has produced a statement the manuscript might
make. Use `templates/claim-note-template.md`.

Include:

- Claim text and claim type.
- Evidence status and confidence.
- Supporting sources with citekeys and locators.
- Counter-sources or counterevidence.
- Safer wording.
- The next research task.

Do not mark a claim as ready because it sounds right. Claims need a visible path
back to source notes, citekeys, locators, and relevant counterevidence.
