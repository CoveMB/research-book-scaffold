# Case studies

Bounded case notes with evidence and limits.

Use this folder for cases, examples, episodes, organizations, sites, or events
that may support a claim or chapter.

Include the case boundary, chronology, relevant context, source trail, comparison
logic, interpretive limits, and unresolved questions. Note why the case is being
used and what it cannot support.

Do not generalize from a case without marking the basis for comparison and the
counter-cases or missing cases that would weaken the use.
