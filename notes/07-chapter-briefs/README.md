# Chapter briefs

Section plans, evidence placement, and missing support.

Use this folder as the bridge from research notes to manuscript drafting.

Include chapter or section purpose, central question, provisional thesis,
argument sequence, evidence placement, likely sources, counterarguments,
definitions to track, and gaps to keep visible in the draft.

Do not draft directly from a brief unless the linked notes, claim records, and
citekeys support the claims being moved into prose.
