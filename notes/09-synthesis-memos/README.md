# Synthesis memos

Provisional synthesis, counterevidence, uncertainty, and next steps.

Use this folder when several source notes, matrices, or literature maps are
ready for cautious cross-source interpretation.

Include the question being synthesized, strongest evidence, strongest
counterevidence, corpus limits, uncertainty, manuscript implications, and next
steps. Link back to the notes and research records that support the synthesis.

Do not treat a synthesis memo as final proof. Convert supported conclusions into
claim notes with evidence status before drafting them as manuscript claims.
